Design system

Civic Clarity

A restrained, editorial system for a public-data utility. Calm authority over flash; data as a first-class citizen; trust signals built into every component. Light theme, WCAG-AA targets, tabular figures throughout.

Color

A dominant deep-teal “ink” for trust, warm neutrals to carry data density, and a single warm-clay accent reserved for CTAs and emphasis. True red is held back for SFF and abuse flags only.
