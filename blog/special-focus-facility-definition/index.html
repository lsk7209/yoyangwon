What families often misunderstand about CMS

The common mistake is treating a public data point as a live bedside report. Public records are published on a schedule, and they may describe a past survey date. That does not make them unimportant. It means the reader should check dates, repetition, and whether later records show improvement.

Another mistake is ignoring resident fit. Special Focus Facility definition may matter differently for short-term rehab, long-term care, dementia support, high fall risk, or a Medicaid-pending admission. The same record can carry different weight depending on the resident's needs.
