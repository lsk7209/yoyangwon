How are “Local percentile” and “Trend” derived?

Local percentile ranks a facility’s overall rating against all CMS-certified nursing homes in the same county. A higher percentile means the facility scores better than that share of nearby peers.

Trend plots the overall star rating across the last six published quarters. It is a visual summary of the official rating history — not a separate score. See the methodology page for the full formula and update cadence.

Reading this comparison

Star ratings run 1–5. For staffing hours and percentile, higher is better. For deficiencies, fines, and turnover, lower is better — the tool accounts for direction automatically.
