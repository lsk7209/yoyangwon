About NH-Data

NH-Data is an independent public-data utility that makes CMS nursing home ratings, staffing, inspection findings, and enforcement records easier to read and compare.

What we publish

We organize public CMS nursing home data into facility profiles, location listings, comparison views, enforcement summaries, and plain-language guides. The current implementation uses illustrative sample records until production CMS ETL is connected.

Editorial principles

We use neutral wording, source stamps, correction links, and methodology notes. Enforcement findings are described as official records, not as unsourced judgments about a facility.

Independence

NH-Data does not sell nursing home placement, referral leads, or facility rankings. Monetization is planned through Google AdSense Auto Ads only after the site has enough public, useful, and source-based content.
