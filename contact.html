Contact the Caregos editorial team

Caregos maintains nursing home comparison pages using public CMS data. Use this page for source questions, correction requests, privacy questions, and feedback about how our facility data is presented.

For record corrections, include the facility name, state, the page URL, the CMS field or inspection item involved, and the public source that supports the change. This helps us separate source-data updates from interpretation questions.

Caregos does not provide medical, legal, placement, or benefits advice. If your question is urgent or facility-specific, contact the facility, long-term care ombudsman, state survey agency, or a qualified professional.

Correction requests may also be started from the corrections page. Privacy questions are handled under our privacy policy.

What to expect

We review messages for data quality, source traceability, and site reliability. We cannot guarantee an individual response to every message, but correction reports with clear public-source evidence are prioritized.
