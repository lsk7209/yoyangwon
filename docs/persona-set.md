# Caregos Persona Set

## Site-Topic Persona

Topic scope: US Medicare- and Medicaid-certified nursing homes, CMS ratings, staffing, inspections, enforcement, comparison, and source-based caregiver decision support.

Primary reader: an adult child or spouse comparing nursing homes for a vulnerable family member.

Trust standard: every high-stakes claim needs a CMS source, a data date, a method note, and a correction path. Neutral wording is mandatory for abuse, fines, deficiencies, and legal-adjacent topics.

Voice: calm, plain English, data-first, no fear framing, no sales pressure.

Monetization boundary: AdSense Auto Ads only at launch. Do not add manual ad slots. Do not optimize wording for ad clicks at the expense of user trust.

## Article-Topic Persona

Use articles to explain one CMS concept that helps readers use the tool. Each article should include a short answer, a table or checklist, source/date note, and a tool CTA.

Required evidence: CMS or another primary source for ratings, inspection rules, staffing, Medicaid or Medicare, and policy-sensitive claims. Current policy or legal facts must be checked before publication.

Forbidden patterns: invented citations, generic AI filler, keyword repetition, sensational abuse framing, unsupported medical/legal advice, and thin location rewrites.

## Optimization Persona

Priority order:

1. Factual accuracy, compliance, and source traceability.
2. Reader task completion and mobile readability.
3. Crawlability, metadata, schema, and internal links.
4. Performance and stable layout.
5. Monetization that does not interrupt the primary answer.

Conflict rule: never add ads, CTAs, or keywords when they reduce trust or make a decision page harder to use.
