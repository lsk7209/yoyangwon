Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Caregos handles information related to site visits, correction requests, analytics, and Google AdSense Auto Ads.

Information we collect

Most pages can be read without submitting personal information. If you use the correction form, we may receive the facility name, correction details, and optional contact email you provide.

Analytics and cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand page performance, search traffic, and navigation patterns. Google AdSense Auto Ads may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads after the site is approved.

Public data

Nursing home ratings, staffing, inspection, and enforcement records are based on public CMS data. We do not publish private resident medical information.

Contact and corrections

To request a correction or privacy-related update, use the contact page or the corrections page.
